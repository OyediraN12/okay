Sanitation Audit

Electricity is available everywhere, we often take it for granted and forget how dangerous it can be. Due to growing concern for public safety and the increased complexity of today’s fixed electrical installations in domestic, commercial and industrial premises, demand of electrical safety inspections is increasing.

All electrical installations must be maintained in an excellent condition and the most important requirement of such maintenance is periodic electrical inspection and testing. Main purpose of this inspection is to ensure the safety of your electrical system and find problems.

An electrical safety inspection totally tests the entire electrical system to ensure that all wires, systems, and modules get together the legitimate safety norms.

Electrical Inspection Is Needed

CDG is a third-party electrical safety inspection company complying with the ISO 17020 standard.

CDG’s Electrical Safety Inspection Report will focus on installations and safe work practices at your site based on the International Electrical Code, NFPA 70E, NFPA 70B, and OSHA 1910.331-335.

Why Electrical Safety Inspection /Audit is Required?
  • Helps in achieving compliance with all relevant electrical regulations.
  • Ensures that premises has required electrical safety for staff & visitors.
  • Enable better planning of maintenance & repair programmes to spread costs.
  • Able to save cost through early identification of loop holes and potential breakdowns
  • It helps you in deciding periodic preventive maintenance schedule.
  • Our independent electrical safety audit will give you complete peace of mind.

An electrical safety inspection in performed by CDG’s skilled electricians will thoroughly and accurately identify all potential electrical problems in your premises. We conduct electrical safety inspection of existing buildings & under construction buildings as well. We also conduct electrical safety audit / inspection of a machine to ensure it is electrically safe. We are conducting electrical safety trainings worldwide..
